In Costa Rica there are currently hundreds Independent Artisans and Designers which are creating very high quality products and services. As an example we have several high skilled teams and individuals creating state of art Jewelry, Clothes, Leather Bags, Toys, Art Pieces, Sculptures, Organic Food, Bakeries and several other examples which are just part of this spread variety of goods and services. Unfortunately the Costa Rican's don't have a reliable e-commerce Plattform which would enable them to sell and promote their own services and goods.

Currently the Costa Ricans rely on workarounds in order to promote the products and services, they are mostly taking advantage of Social Networks to promote, market and even facilitate the selling of the products. Unfortunately they have to work based on Photo Albums as Product Catalogs, Use of Google Forms to take orders and the most critical problem, they rely on cash transactions or wire transfers since there's no easy way to implement a Credit Card System.

Current Limitations

Due to these reasons the Online Business for most them is not a primary source of incomes, currently their main source of income is via having a Booth in Art Fairs, Carnivals and Street Markets. Having these Booths requires a lot of pre-payments and most of time a preferred spot by the government who usually organizes the events. One of the issues that these fairs is that they are seasonal and not very frequent, there might be 3 of these a year, each of them would have around 1.6 MM visitors during 1 Week and most of them would walk around the booths. The Biggest issue with these seasonal events is the lack of follow up, the consumers don't have the opportunity to find a central place where all the products that they might offer would be available for purchase, not everyone carry the cash to buy immediately. As example we can take the Example of Cafe Britt who are making the difference of selling Coffee from Costa Rica to the world and are breaking their own estimates and reaching several locations around the world through globalization, they keep having a steady 15% growth of online sales every year. Costa Rica has the opportunity of delivering quality, innovative and state of the art products to the Costa Rican and as well to the world.
